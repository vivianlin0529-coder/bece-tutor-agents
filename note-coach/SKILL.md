# Note-Taking Mastery Skill

## Overview
The Note-Taking Mastery skill equips students with effective note-taking strategies.

## Instructions
1. Introduce various note-taking techniques such as the Cornell method and mind mapping.
2. Encourage students to practice these techniques during lectures.
3. Review and refine their notes regularly.

## Teaching Formats
- Instructional Videos
- Hands-On Workshops

## Routing Logic
- For disorganized note-takers, focus on structure methods.
- Encourage creative formats for students showing high engagement.

## Examples
- Demonstrating a mind map for a historical event.

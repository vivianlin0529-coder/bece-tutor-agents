# Motivation & Achievement Tracking Skill

## Overview
The Motivation & Achievement Tracking skill implements a badge system to encourage progress.

## Instructions
1. Design a badge system that rewards various levels of achievement.
2. Explain the criteria for earning badges to students.
3. Track achievements and provide feedback on progress.

## Teaching Formats
- Incentive Workshops
- Monthly Review Meetings

## Routing Logic
- Utilize the badge system for students lacking motivation.
- Provide customized goals for high-achievers to keep them engaged.

## Examples
- Awarding badges for completed learning milestones.

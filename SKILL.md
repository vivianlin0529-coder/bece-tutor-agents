# Video Tutorial Coach Functionality

## Overview
The Video Tutorial Coach is designed to enhance the learning experience by providing comprehensive video analysis, note extraction, and quiz generation features.

## Features

### 1. Video Analysis
- **Objective**: Provide insights and enhance comprehension of the video content.
- **Functionality**:
  - Detect and summarize key topics discussed in the video.
  - Analyze speech patterns and tone to offer feedback on delivery.
  - Extract important visual elements, such as text, charts, and images.

### 2. Note Extraction
- **Objective**: Facilitate important note-taking during or after video viewing.
- **Functionality**:
  - Users can generate structured notes based on video content.
  - Highlight key points, quotes, and visuals.
  - Allow customization of notes with user annotations.

### 3. Quiz Generation
- **Objective**: Assess comprehension of the video material.
- **Functionality**:
  - Create a 10-question quiz based on video content.
  - Questions will vary in format, including multiple-choice, true/false, and short answer.
  - Provide immediate feedback on quiz performance, highlighting areas for improvement.

## Conclusion
The Video Tutorial Coach combines these functionalities to provide a holistic learning tool that adapts to various learning styles and needs.
